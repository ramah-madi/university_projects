#### NAO as a warehouse operative...
[1] Aim: Make NAO recognize the content of boxes (e.g. when used as a warehouse 
robot).

[2]You need these boxes: NAOMark, “Switch Case” , “Text Edit” ,“Say Text” .

[3]The Procedures:
• Drag the boxes NAOMark, “Switch Case”, “Say Text”, and three Text Edit boxes onto your flow diagram panel.

• Connect them as shown in the screenshot.

• Write the numbers of the three different NAOmarks in the “Switch Case” box.

• Now all you have to do is enter the content of the boxes, for example "Fruit","Muesli", and "Bread" into the Text Edit boxes.
